App Features

Our app is designed to streamline the canteen experience for both students and canteen staff. Below are the key features for each user:

Student Features

  • Personalized Sign-in: Easy login for students using their campus credentials.
  • Menu: Browse the full canteen menu, including pricing and item details.
  • Cart: Add items to your cart for a seamless ordering experience.
  • Online Payment: Secure payments powered by Razorpay.
  • Profile: Manage your account details and view order history.
  • Connect with Canteen: Easily connect to your canteen using a unique code.

Canteen Features

  • Personalized Sign-in: Secure sign-in for canteen staff to manage their operations.
  • Menu Management: Add, update, or remove items from the menu in real-time.
  • Order Management: View incoming orders and accept them instantly.
  • Real-Time Notifications: Receive notifications for new orders, updates, and more.
  • Profile: Manage canteen details and preferences through the profile page.
  • Inventory Management: Track and update inventory to ensure smooth operations.
